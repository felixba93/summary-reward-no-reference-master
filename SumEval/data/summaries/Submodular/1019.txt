The case study method, with its use of multiple data collection methods and analysis techniques, provides researchers with opportunities to triangulate data in order to strengthen the research findings and conclusions. On the other hand, it was suggested by Yasmin that there are some aspects of identity and experience (such as trauma) that can not be spoken and that methods like these may have the capacity to reveal; to provide a way for researchers to witness the 'unspeakable'. Issues of resolution and accuracy need to be considered for both the measured data and the model set-up.
