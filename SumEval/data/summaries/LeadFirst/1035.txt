
Here's how you can help your child cope with feelings of depression. 
Difficulty: Average Time Required: Varies Here's How: Reassure your child that depression is not something to be ashamed of nor is he crazy. 
All of us feel sad in response to sad situations. 
Some of us have an illness that makes it harder for us to recover from sadness. 
Give your child the right to have these feelings. 
Children can easily get the idea that it's not okay to feel depressed and start to hide their feelings rather than deal with them in a healthy way.
