
Carol Fraser Boles, the Literacy Specialist for the National Writing for Children Center, is a wonderful resource person for parents who are teaching their children to read or acting as a facilitator in the process. 
Carol is a former public school K-12 Reading Specialist and special education teacher, and she has written many articles on literacy. 
She also offers suggestions for books and resources on her website at www.carolfraserboles.com, as well as a page of 'literacy lingo' terms that are very helpful.
