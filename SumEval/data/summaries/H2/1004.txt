Symptoms of depression in children may not be obvious .
Treatment of major depression is as effective for children as it is for adults.
Untreated depression can have important consequences for the child's well-being.
Depression is harmful whether or not a child has a chronic disease.
Therapy combined with antidepressants is thought to produce the best outcomes in children with depression.
Depression is not just an illness of the mind.
Your child's doctor will rule out any other physical causes of your child's symptoms.
Talk to your child to see how he or she is feeling.
