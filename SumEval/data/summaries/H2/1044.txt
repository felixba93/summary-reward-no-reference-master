MANY parents are ''in complete denial'' about their child's weight, obesity specialists say.
Parents need to establish healthy habits early in life.
Overweight children are very likely to become overweight adults.
Parents should be encouraged to teach and role model healthy lifestyle behaviours for their young children.
Obese children are subject to teasing and can be socially isolated by their peers.
 Limit the amount of time your child engages in sedentary activities.
 Ensure that your child has a trustworthy adult confidant.
Eating Disorders are not uncommon in our society today.
