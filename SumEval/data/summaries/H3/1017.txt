Federal loans do not require students to have a good credit history.
A: Students with a bad credit history can apply for loans that do not require credit checks.
About No Cosigner Student Loans No Cosigner Student Loans are available for students who have an established credit record with a good credit score.
Private lenders also require applicants to have good credit history and a co-signer in case of student loans.
