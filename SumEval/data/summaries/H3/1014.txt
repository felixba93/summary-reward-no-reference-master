This surge in teen smoking is due to a number of factors, including the targeting of minors in tobacco industry advertising, coupled with an alarming decline of concern among teenagers about the dangers of cigarette use.
Like many other teenage disciplinary issues, convincing teens to stop smoking can be a challenge.
We all know that peer pressure is one of the most common reasons teenagers start smoking, by allowing a forum and room for peer dialogue could be the right medicine a teen needs to stop smoking or not start at all.
